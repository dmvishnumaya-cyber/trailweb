Puthuparambil Sree Bhadrakali Vishnumaya Temple is peacefully located in Minalur, Athani, Thrissur, Kerala, offering devotees a serene and spiritually uplifting destination that is easily accessible from Thrissur city and nearby towns. Surrounded by the beauty of Kerala's countryside, the temple welcomes devotees throughout the year to seek the divine blessings of Sree Vishnumaya Swamy and Sree Bhadrakali Devi in a sacred atmosphere filled with faith, peace, and devotion.
