Puthuparambil Sree Bhadrakali Vishnumaya Temple is a sacred and spiritually vibrant temple located at Minalur, Athani, Thrissur, Kerala. Dedicated to the divine blessings of Sree Vishnumaya Swamy and Sree Bhadrakali Devi, the temple is a place where devotees gather throughout the year to seek protection, prosperity, peace, and divine grace. Surrounded by the calm beauty of Kerala's countryside, the temple provides a serene atmosphere for prayer, meditation, and worship. Whether you are visiting with your family, attending special poojas, or seeking spiritual guidance, every devotee is welcomed with devotion and respect. We invite you to visit the temple, participate in the sacred rituals, and experience the divine presence that has inspired generations of devotees.
