Special Poojas

Participate in the sacred rituals of Puthuparambil Sree Bhadrakali Vishnumaya Temple and seek divine blessings for prosperity, protection, peace, and spiritual well-being.

Sacred Offerings

Every pooja performed at Puthuparambil Sree Bhadrakali Vishnumaya Temple is conducted according to Kerala's ancient Tantric traditions with utmost devotion and spiritual purity. Devotees participate in these sacred offerings to receive divine grace, overcome life's obstacles, attain prosperity, experience healing, and strengthen their spiritual connection with Sree Vishnumaya Swamy and Sree Bhadrakali Devi.

Amavasya Mahashakthi Pooja

Performed on every Amavasya, this highly powerful pooja invokes the blessings of the Divine Mother for removing doshas, overcoming obstacles, ensuring family prosperity, spiritual protection, and success in every aspect of life.

Vishnumaya Special Pooja

A sacred ritual dedicated to Sree Vishnumaya Swamy seeking divine protection, victory over enemies, relief from difficulties, success in business, peaceful family life, and overall well-being.

Bhadrakali Devi Pooja

A powerful worship offered to Sree Bhadrakali Devi for courage, protection from evil influences, good health, mental strength, and divine guidance throughout life's journey.

Archana & Deepam

Devotees may offer Archana and sacred lamps to express gratitude, pray for fulfilled wishes, seek peace of mind, and receive the everlasting blessings of the Divine.

Special Festival Poojas

During annual festivals and auspicious occasions, elaborate poojas, traditional rituals, and devotional ceremonies are conducted with great grandeur, attracting devotees from across Kerala.

Personal Sankalpam Pooja

Devotees may offer special poojas in their own names and family names seeking blessings for marriage, education, career, health, childbirth, financial growth, and overall happiness.

Book Your Offering

Devotees wishing to participate in any special pooja may contact the temple office in advance for booking details, pooja timings, offerings, and special festival schedules. Every offering made with sincere devotion becomes a sacred prayer seeking the eternal blessings of Sree Vishnumaya Swamy and Sree Bhadrakali Devi.
